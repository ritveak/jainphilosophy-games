Clarifying Doubts

Short answers that help translate the laboratory into the way you make choices.

Does this mean everything is predetermined?

Not exactly. The lab shows multiple conditions matter. Some outcomes depend on factors beyond effort, but inward posture remains a meaningful choice.

Where does free will exist?

Free will appears in how you show up. You can choose effort, direction, and whether you become attached to the result.

Why can effort fail?

Because success requires many aligned causes. Effort is vital, but it is only one part of the full causal system.

How is this useful today?

It helps reduce attachment to outcomes, make better decisions, and notice when your inner awareness is still free even when the world is not fully aligned.
